Masterclasses

Masterclasses are a cross between a private lesson and a performance: You have the opportunity to work one-on-one with a professional, but in the presence of an audience. Below are several videos of me in masterclasses recieving feedback.

Masterclass with Kersten McCall

In this masterclass I performed the Daphnis et Chloe flute solo with flute trio accompaniment for Kersten McCall, Principal Flutist of the Royal Concertgebouw Orchestra.

Masterclass with James Galway

I was invited by the Galways to perform at their summer festival in Weggis, Switzerland. In this video I am working on fundamentals in tone with Sir James. The entire festival was a learning experience I will never forget!
