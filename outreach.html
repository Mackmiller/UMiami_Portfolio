Music Outreach

From a young age I have enjoyed giving back to the community through music. I have performed in nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, and hospitals for patients, their families, and staff. I also enjoy teaching my own private studio of flute students. Another outreach opportunity I have been very grateful for these past three years is my volunteer work with the New Horizons Band as a flute instructor. The New Horizons Band consists of beginnning and intermediate adult musicians who are interested in learning an instrument. This is a community band sponsored by UM, and they meet every Saturday morning from 9-11:30am for sectionals and group rehearsal.

New Horizons Band

In the video above, I am leading the New Horizon Band flute choir's debut performance at their holiday concert. My goals for the group were for them to be able to give one another constructive critism, improve aural skills by blending and focusing on intonation, and we also worked on being able to keep time without a conductor.
